Chicken Masala Recipe

Description

Chicken Masala translates to chicken cooked with spices where "masala" means spices.

Masala involves sauteing onions, tomatoes, spices and herbs. Marinated chicken is sauteed and cooked in this masala. This is a quick and easy version that uses basic ingredients and won't break your bank!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3/4 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 small bay leaf
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cup onions
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 3/4 teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 handful coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Add chicken to a large mixing bowl. Add ginger garlic paste, turmeric, red chilli powder, salt, yoghurt, and garam masala.
  2. Cover and set aside for about 45 mins to 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in pan. Add whole spices (cloves, cinnamon, cardomoms and bay leaf).
  4. Add onions and saute on a medium heat for 5-6 mins.
  5. Reduce heat and saute for a few more minutes until the onions turn golden.
  6. Add ginger garlic paste and saute for a minute, until the raw smell of ginger garlic goes off.
  7. Pour the tomato puree and add salt, red chilli powder, and garam masala.
  8. Saute this until the mixture smells aromatic and releases oil. This takes 2-3 mins.
  9. Add marinated chicken and saute for 4-5 mins on a high heat.
  10. Reduce to low heat and cook covered for 5 mins.
  11. Add coriander leaves and stir thoroughly.
  12. Cover the pan and cook on a low heat until chicken is soft and tender.
